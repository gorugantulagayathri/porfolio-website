fun facts about SVT:-

SEVENTEEN (SVT) is a 13-member K-pop group known for being self-producing idols, with members actively involved in writing, composing, and choreographing their music. Their name represents 13 members, 3 units (hip-hop, vocal, and performance), and 1 team. Debuting in 2015, they are famous for their powerful synchronization and teamwork, which sets them apart in performances. Their fandom is called CARAT, symbolizing how fans help them shine. SEVENTEEN is also loved for their fun and chaotic personalities, especially showcased in their variety show Going Seventeen, and they have achieved massive global success with million-selling albums and record-breaking achievements, making them one of the most influential K-pop groups today. 💎
